Our Story

Petals & Blooms started with a simple truth: a girl (Shaine Unabia) who found magic in every petal and a man (Mark Gabriel Indefenzo) who wanted to capture that magic for her. Built on a foundation of unconditional love, this shop was created by him as a sanctuary for her passion.

Today, our team has grown to 3 dedicated members Dan Kenneth Bayro, Stephanie Mendoza, and Gilianah Savedia , but the heart of the shop remains the same—a tribute to the beauty of flowers and the strength of the bond that started it all.
