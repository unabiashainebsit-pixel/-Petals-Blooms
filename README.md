🌸 Project Concept

Petals & Blooms is a boutique florist website designed to showcase and sell a variety of floral arrangements while emphasizing the emotional meaning behind each flower. The concept of the website is centered on the idea that flowers are not just decorative items, but symbols of love, appreciation, and human connection.

The website aims to provide users with a smooth and visually pleasing experience through a clean layout, elegant typography, and interactive features such as a product gallery, add-to-cart system, and feedback section. Each section of the website is carefully structured to guide users from exploring floral products to understanding the story behind the shop.

At its core, the project is inspired by a personal story of unconditional love, where the business was created as a way to support and celebrate someone’s passion for flowers. This emotional foundation makes the website more meaningful, transforming it from a simple online shop into a platform that expresses sincerity, care, and connection.

Overall, the project combines functionality and storytelling, allowing users not only to purchase flowers but also to experience the deeper message that every flower tells a story.
